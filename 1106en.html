The Snow is Here

For a few weeks now, the long and warm summer has ended, and autumn has arrived. The leaves are changing colors, and it's raining a lot.

A tree with yellow leaves next to the bus stop at the university.

It rained for several days in a row, and on Friday, suddenly, one could see snow on the mountaintops. Even though it's 10 degrees and raining in the valley, it's snowing on the mountain peaks. Not surprising when you think about it, but it was not what I expected.

Snow on the top of a mountain.

Now it has been around zero degrees even here in the valley, so I expect the snow cover to slowly make its way down along the mountains.

Mountains far away covered in snow.
